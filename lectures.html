Course Lectures

MATH 113: Calculus I

Lecture 1: Pre-Calculus Review (Lines)

A foundational review of lines, real numbers, and the core concept of a function.

Lecture 2: Functions & Models (Pre-Calculus Review)

Exploring the essential families of functions: polynomials, exponential, logarithmic, and trigonometric functions.

Lecture 3: An Intuitive Approach to Tangents and Velocity

Introducing the two fundamental problems that lead to the concept of the derivative.

Lecture 4: Limits An Introduction

Developing an intuitive understanding of limits using numerical and graphical evidence.

Lecture 5: Limit Properties

Moving from estimation to exact calculation with the algebraic properties of limits.

Lecture 6: Continuity

Defining what it means for a function to be continuous and exploring the Intermediate Value Theorem.
